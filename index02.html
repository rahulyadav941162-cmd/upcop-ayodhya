CCTNS & UPCOP Support Desk

Escalation Protocol

  • Verify that the application's pending status is correctly updated in CCTNS.
  • Ensure the concerned Verification Officer (पूछताछ अधिकारी) has been notified.
  • For urgent social media (Twitter/X) or IGRS portal complaints, share the Application Reference Number directly with the support helpdesk.

IGRS & Twitter Complaint Handling

Public Grievance Resolution
Citizens often file complaints regarding delayed verification certificates via Twitter/X tagging senior officers, or through the official IGRS UP portal.
Strict Timelines
Social media complaints require rapid action. When a complaint is forwarded to the district, the CCTNS Nodal Desk must investigate the application immediately.
Certificate Generation
Once character verifications are completed by the local PS and verified by DCRB/LIU, certificates are generated digitally. Ensure reports are synced correctly.

About CCTNS & UPCOP Nodal Cell

CCTNS & Digital Verification Management Team, Ayodhya Police

The CCTNS (Crime and Criminal Tracking Network & Systems) Nodal Cell of Ayodhya District Police is dedicated to streamlining citizen services, ensuring swift processing of digital verifications, and addressing public grievances with maximum transparency. Our primary mission is the timely disposal of character verifications and certificate requests submitted through the official UPCOP mobile application.
